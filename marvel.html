Marvel Cinematic Universe Movie Guide

Phase 1

  • Iron Man (2008) - Tony Stark becomes Iron Man.
  • The Incredible Hulk (2008) - Bruce Banner controls his rage as the Hulk.
  • Iron Man 2 (2010) - Tony Stark faces rivals and a new villain.
  • Thor (2011) - A banished Asgardian prince learns humility on Earth.
  • Captain America: The First Avenger (2011) - Steve Rogers becomes Captain America and fights Red Skull.
  • The Avengers (2012) - Earth's mightiest heroes unite to defeat Loki.

Phase 2

  • Iron Man 3 (2013) - Tony Stark battles a terrorist called the Mandarin.
  • Thor: The Dark World (2013) - Thor fights an enemy who threatens the Nine Realms.
  • Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014) - Captain America and Black Widow fight a mysterious force within S.H.I.E.L.D.
